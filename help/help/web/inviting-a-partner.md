# Inviting a Partner

_Written by AI_

Invite your accountability partner from the dashboard's **Partners** section.

If you enable **Can view data**, the web app will try to attach your encrypted
sharing key automatically when you send the invite.

- If your partner already has an account, the encrypted key is attached right
  away.
- If they have not signed up yet, you can invite them by email first and then
  use **Confirm partner** later to attach the encrypted key after their account
  is created.

Your partner never needs you to manually tell them an E2EE password.
