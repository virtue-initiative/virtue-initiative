The Virtue Initiative

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Overview

The Virtue Initiative is a non-profit pornography accountability application. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. We do not sell, advertise with, or monetize your personal data in any way.

2. Data we collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — your email address and an optional display name, provided at sign-up.
  • Screenshots — periodic screenshots captured by the The Virtue Initiative client installed on your devices. These are uploaded to our servers and stored for the retention period you configure (default: 30 days).
  • Device metadata — the name, platform, and activity timestamps of each device you register.
  • Activity logs — records of capture events (including any missed captures) associated with your devices.
  • Partner relationships — the email addresses of accountability partners you invite, and the permissions you grant them.

3. How we use your data

Your data is used solely to provide the The Virtue Initiative service:

  • Screenshots and activity logs are made accessible to the accountability partners you have explicitly invited and granted access to.
  • Account and device data is used to authenticate you and operate the service.
  • We do not analyze, process, or view your screenshots for any purpose other than storing and serving them to you and your authorized partners.

4. Data retention

Screenshots and logs are automatically deleted after your configured retention period (default 30 days, adjustable in account settings). You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at help@virtueinitiative.org.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Your screenshots and logs are only accessible to you and the accountability partners you have explicitly authorized. We use Cloudflare infrastructure (R2 object storage, Workers) to store and serve data; Cloudflare's own privacy policy governs their handling of infrastructure-level data.

6. Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS. Screenshots are stored in a private object store accessible only via authenticated API requests. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plaintext.

7. Children

The Virtue Initiative is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

8. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at help@virtueinitiative.org. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at help@virtueinitiative.org.
