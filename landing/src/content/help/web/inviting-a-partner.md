# Inviting a Partner

Invite your accountability partner from the dashboard's **Partners** section.

If you enable **Can view data**, the server will include that partner in future
batch access once they accept the invite.

- Existing encrypted batches stay as they were when uploaded.
- New encrypted batches are wrapped for the owner and each accepted partner
  automatically.

There is no separate key-confirmation step.
