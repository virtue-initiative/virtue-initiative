BePure

A free, open-source pornography accountability tool.

About

BePure is a non-profit pornography accountability application. It runs quietly in the background on your devices and takes periodic screenshots, which are shared with one or more accountability partners of your choosing. Partners can review your activity and help you stay on track with your personal commitments.

There is no subscription, no advertising, and no data sold to third parties. The project is community-funded and source code is publicly available.

You are always in control: you choose your partners, configure what they can see, set how frequently screenshots are taken, and decide how long your data is retained.

How it works

  1. Install the BePure client on one or more of your devices.
  2. Create an account and invite an accountability partner by email.
  3. The client runs in the background, capturing periodic screenshots and uploading them securely.
  4. Your partner logs in to the web portal to review your activity log and screenshots.
