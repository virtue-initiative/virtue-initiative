About

The Virtue Initiative is an organization that offers a free accountability app for your devices. We were founded when we saw the lack of a non-commercial and free app in this space. While we believe accountability is something worth paying for, we do not want money to be the reason someone stays stuck in a pornography addiction.

Our mission is to develop free technology and resources to support ending pornography addiction. We know what it's like to feel stuck, and we want to provide tools to support you, and we don't want the price to be yet another excuse not to deal with pornography. It's already hard enough.

Our vision is to see the Christian church and the world freed from pornography. There are billions of dollars made by keeping men and women stuck in addiction. For many people, it starts from a young age and it's hard to give up, even if they desperately want to. We want to provide tools to provide support and make it easier.

The Virtue Initiative began in February 2026 when we started looking at accountability apps. There were a few options but most of them cost around $15/month. One of us being a full time student, we knew that could be a blocker and we thought we could do better. Our goal is to fundraise all our funds and offer the app for free. We believe this is a big enough issue that people will be willing to donate and support the mission of developing technology and resources for ending pornography addiction.

Hopefully you're willing to join us on this mission. If you've been or know someone who's been struggling to break free from porn, sign up now or send this to them. You might also want to read this page on tips to beat the addiction.

If you wish to support us monetarily, you can donate (at some point). If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at help@virtueinitiative.org

How it works

  1. Create an account
  2. Install the The Virtue Initiative client on one or more of your devices.
  3. Invite an accountability partner by email. They will need to create an account.
  4. The client runs in the background, capturing periodic blurry screenshots and uploading them securely.
  5. Your partner logs in to the web portal to review your activity log and screenshots.
